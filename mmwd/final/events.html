    Here Is a Breakdown Of All Our Events!

    • Craft-an-Egg Workshop
      Decorate your own magical egg — some might even "hatch" with special effects and prizes!

    • Yoshi's Fruit Mix-Up
      A fruit-matching game show with live Yoshis as hosts — audience participation, music, and foam fruit prizes galore!

    • Dino Dance Bash
      A nightly neon-lit party where Yoshis and guests dance together in rhythm-based mini-games.

    • Saddle-Up Safari
      Ride a Yoshi around a guided course that recreates famous levels from Yoshi's Island.

    • The Great Yoshi Egg Hunt
      Hidden eggs around the island contain treats, clues, and sometimes a chance to meet rare colored Yoshis!

    • Yoshipalooza Parade
      A spectacular music and light show featuring dozens of Yoshis on floats, synchronized dancing, and bubble cannons.

    • Starlight Snack Fest
      Nighttime food festival with glow-in-the-dark fruit treats, themed desserts, and Yoshi-shaped everything

    • Color Splash Carnival
      A vibrant festival celebrating Yoshi colors with paint fountains, color races, and themed mini-games.
