    • About Yoshi Resort!

    An Egg-cellent Escape Awaits! Welcome to Yoshi's Resort, where island magic, colorful adventures, and dinosaur-sized fun come together in the most egg-ceptional getaway this side of the Mushroom Kingdom! Nestled on the sunny shores of Yoshi's Island, this resort is a dreamy paradise crafted by Yoshi and friends just for you—yes, YOU! From the moment you hatch into our world, you'll be greeted with friendly faces, tropical breezes, and the unmistakable scent of fresh melons and star fruits wafting through the air. Hop aboard a Blargg Boat, bounce across flower-filled meadows, or take a scenic ride on the iconic Yoshi Egg Tram—painted in all your favorite spotted patterns, of course! What's waiting for you at Yoshi's Resort? Egg-Collecting Adventures: Join daily scavenger hunts across the resort grounds—hidden surprise eggs might contain golden coins, mini-games, or even rare souvenirs! Melon Splash Lagoon: Slide, swim, and splash in our tropical waterpark, complete with Shy Guy fountains and flutter-jump diving boards. Crafted Cove Spa: Inspired by Yoshi's Crafted World, this relaxing oasis features cardboard-cutout saunas, yarn ball massages, and origami tea rooms. ️ Snug-a-Saurus Suites: Sleep like a baby Yoshi in our themed bungalows—each room styled after iconic Yoshi game levels, from Jungle Jammin' to Snowy Snack Peaks! Whether you're a longtime fan or a curious newcomer, Yoshi's Resort is your go-to destination for whimsical wonder, cozy charm, and just the right amount of egg-venture. We believe life's more fun with a little flutter jump, a big appetite for fruit, and a lot of heart. So grab your favorite colored Yoshi (or become one yourself), and get ready to stomp, roll, splash, and explore a vacation like no other. Yoshi's Resort – Where Every Day is a Yoshi's Story!
