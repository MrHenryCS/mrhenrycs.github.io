    • Our Featured Attractions!


    Eggventure Coaster

    A high-speed ride through volcanoes, waterfalls, and lush jungles — all inside a giant Yoshi egg-shaped cart!

    Yoshi Lagoon Safari

    A scenic boat ride through glowing caves and tropical bays, guided by snorkeling Yoshis who love to splash.

    Rainbow Slide Ridge

    Race down color-changing waterslides built into a vibrant cliffside, with views of the entire island.

    Egg Rolling Derby

    A competitive (but hilarious) obstacle course where guests roll giant decorative eggs alongside their favorite Yoshi partners.

    Flutter Jump Sky Park

    A trampoline and glide zone that mimics the Yoshi flutter jump — bounce between sky platforms with feathered parachutes.

    Crayon Caverns

    A dazzling underground maze where bioluminescent crystals pulse in sync with music, leading to secret treasure eggs.

    Yoshi's Treetop Treat Drop

    Zipline through the jungle while collecting fruit-shaped tokens that can be exchanged for tropical snacks.
