About Us

Family-owned since 1985, Hometown Pizza has been a beloved part of our community. Our secret lies in our award-winning homemade sauce, perfected over generations, and our commitment to fresh, local ingredients.

Each pizza is made with love, from our hand-tossed dough to our premium cheese blend that melts to perfection. Whether you're a fan of classic flavors or adventurous toppings, we have something for everyone!


Our Community

Hometown Pizza isn't just about food – we're about family and community. We proudly sponsor local children's sports teams, school fundraisers, and charity events. Every year, we host a "Pizza for a Cause" night where a portion of our sales go to helping local families in need.

Stop by our shop and check out our "Wall of Champions," featuring photos of the youth teams we've supported over the years!
