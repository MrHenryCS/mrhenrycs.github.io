Our Journey

Easy Bake Oven started as a way for my daughter to bake safely without much help. Our family worked together to create this product through many trails and errors. I'm so glad we could form this and share it with the world. Since our release of this product we have craeted more editions and treat packets for the Easy Bake Ovens.
Our product is immensely popular with young children and teenagers. With this product any kid on any age (over 4) can have a fun and safe time baking adn cooking many types of snacks.
