AP Computer Science A

Mr. Henry Room 211

This class is devoted to teaching students to program in the JAVA language. They will progress through multiple units of learning the feature of java getting to fairly advanced topics.

Some of the projects that students will make throughout the course are Mad Libs, and a fully functioning game of Battleship.

At the end of the school year students will get the oppertunity to take the AP exam to potentially earn you college credit or advanced placement, allowing you to skip introductory courses and potentially graduate early, saving both time and money
